About Us

Mr. Vintage is an independently owned and operated earth friendly record shop located in Seattle, WA. We funded Mr. Vintage because we love music and we love physical media. As big music fans who appreciate audio fidelity and great artwork, we particularly love vinyl but noticed it was getting harder and harder to buy our favorite records without paying reseller prices so we started Mr. Vintage to see if we could help get music into the hands of fans first. We've learned quite a bit since we started and are continuously improving our operations. We've expanded our main warehouse space in Seattle where we house 10,000+ records and we are currently looking for a retail space to compliment our online presence. We treat each record like the collectible it is and use vinyl specific packaging to get your record to you safely.
